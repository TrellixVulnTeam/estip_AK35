CUSTOMER SENTIMENT ANALYSIS

  • It is always necessary to know the feedback from your customers.
  • Virtually conduct a sentiment analysis based on a text input.
  • Predicts an overall sentiment , tone or emotional feeling.
  • Give you a sentiment score.
  • Great tool for companies to know the intent behind the confusing response from a customer.

Benefits of Customer Sentiment Analysis

  • Customer service:
    • Negative reviews have potential to shut down a business .
    • Deal with small issues before they become major issues.
    • Customer feedback and reviews are pivotal when good.
    • When bad , we need a strategy in place to deal with them.
  • Audience Insights:
    • Businesses should know what customers really wanted.
    • Use social media to get information.
    • Keep an eye on how competitors are doing with the situation.
    • Stay ahead of the customer experience game.
