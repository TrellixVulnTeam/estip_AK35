    About BNG:
    • BNG stands for broadband gateway network.
    • This dataset contains data of performance of a broadband gateway network (BNG) device.
    • BNG is access point for subscribers, through which they connect to the broadband network.
    • When a connection is established between BNG device and Customer Premise Equipment (CPE), the subscriber can access the broadband services provided by the Network Service Provider (NSP) .
    • A network management company monitors the performance of each of the BNG devices to provide better services
    • The device records different performance measures (PMs) for every 15 minutes.
